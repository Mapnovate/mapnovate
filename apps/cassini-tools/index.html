Cassini Tools (web) reproduces the desktop plugin's geodetic engine in the browser: Clarke 1858 ellipsoid, position-vector Helmert (EPSG 9606), centroid-reduced least squares with adaptive 3/7-parameter selection, and honest ground round-trip accuracy. Transforms run locally via proj4js — no data leaves your device. Control points are treated at ellipsoidal height 0.
